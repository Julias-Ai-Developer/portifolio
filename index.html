About Me

Building the Future of AI

I'm Julias Muyambi - a passionate AI Researcher and Software Developer from Uganda, focused on building intelligent systems, business platforms, and AI-powered tools that work in the real world.

My work spans AI research, designing and deploying autonomous AI agents, AI modeling, image modeling, Python, Django, Laravel, and full-stack product development. I am especially drawn to agentic AI: systems that can reason, plan, use tools, and complete real tasks with minimal human input.

Beyond AI, I build complete software products for operations and retail: collaboration systems, asset management systems, records management systems, point of sale systems, parking platforms, and customer-facing web applications.

I started as a freelancer, working with different companies and practical client needs. I now also build under Kama Trust while growing as a Microsoft technology learner, AI researcher, and software developer.
