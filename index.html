About Me

I'm Julias Muyambi, an AI and software developer who turns business processes into reliable, intelligent digital tools. I enjoy working closely with organisations to understand how they actually work, then translating those workflows into intuitive software and AI-powered solutions.

I build end-to-end solutions such as Assetlite for asset management, Safe Park for parking management, and Recordlite for digital records, alongside bespoke news and restaurant websites that are fast, mobile-friendly, and easy to update.

Most of my work is web-based, combining clean front-end interfaces with solid back-end logic and databases so that non-technical teams can manage assets, records, and daily operations without friction.

I keep learning new tools and best practices, and I care about building solutions that are maintainable, well-documented, and ready to grow with the businesses that use them.
